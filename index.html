Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about LIC policies, coverage, and benefits. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact our support team.

What types of insurance policies does LIC offer?

LIC offers a wide range of insurance policies, including life insurance, health insurance, retirement plans, endowment policies, child education plans, and more. These policies are designed to cater to different needs, from long-term savings to protection and retirement solutions.

How can I check my LIC policy status?

You can check your LIC policy status online through the LIC portal. Register yourself by providing your policy number and other details. Alternatively, you can visit your nearest LIC office or use the LIC mobile app for policy updates.

How do I pay my LIC premium?

LIC premiums can be paid through various methods such as online payments via the LIC website or mobile app, NEFT, direct debit from your bank account, LIC premium payment centers, or through authorized agents. You can also pay at LIC branches and select banks.

What are the tax benefits of LIC policies?

Premiums paid towards LIC policies are eligible for tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act. The maturity proceeds, including bonuses, are generally tax-free under Section 10(10D), provided certain conditions are met.

What should I do if I miss paying my LIC premium?

If you miss paying your premium within the grace period (usually 30 days for annual and quarterly premiums, and 15 days for monthly premiums), your policy may lapse. You can revive the policy within a specific period by paying the overdue premiums with interest.
